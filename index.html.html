ABOUT

DEBU AUTOMOBILES

With over 15 years of trusted expertise, Debu Automobiles has established itself as a renowned name in the 3-wheeler and e-rickshaw industry. Known for reliability, quality service, and strong customer relationships, we have proudly served thousands of satisfied clients across the region. Over the past year, we’ve taken a significant leap forward by stepping into manufacturing. Today, Debu Automobiles not only deals in top-tier 3-wheelers and e-rickshaws but also designs and manufactures high-performance e-rickshaws and electric loaders that are eco-friendly, efficient, and built for Indian roads.
Why Choose Debu Automobiles?

  • Sales of 3-Wheelers
  • Electric Rickshaw Distribution
  • Custom Loader Manufacturing
  • After-Sales Support
At Debu Automobiles, we’re not just driving vehicles—we’re driving the future of clean, sustainable transport in India.
